What is FiOS®?

FiOS® brings internet, TV and phone connections to your home using a dedicated fiber optic network rather than traditional copper lines. It will change the way you use and enjoy all three services.

You will experience faster internet connections that will keep pace with everything you want to do. You’ll enjoy stunning HD picture and sound on your TV. Your phone calls will be clearer.

FAQ about moving up to FiOS®

What is the installation process?
Our technician will come to your home and take care of everything. The technician will determine the best place to install the FiOS® Optical Network Terminal, make sure the service is working properly and walk you through using your new service.
What is the move-up promotional pricing?
FiOS® Internet 50/50, featuring connection upload/download speeds as fast as 50 Mbps, is available for $45 monthly before taxes and fees. Combine FiOS® Digital Voice with the 50/50 plan for just $65 monthly before taxes and fees. Add FiOS® Custom TV for $105 monthly before taxes and fees. Additional bundles are available. All plans require a one-year term and are subject to early termination fees.
Is there an extra cost for installation?
During the move-up promotion with a one-year agreement, installation is free. Your new FiOS® router to support your internet service is free for 12 months. After the promotion ends, the router will be yours for a $10 monthly rental fee.
How does FiOS® Digital Voice compare to my current voice service?
Your FiOS® fiber connection—with crystal-clear voice quality—includes unlimited voice-calling to Canada, Puerto Rico, U.S. territories and Mexico, and also provides more ways to manage your calls. Use your phone, your computer, your TV or the MyFrontier mobile app.
Does it work the same as my current voice service?
You can use your current corded or cordless phones and have most of the popular calling features included. FiOS® Digital Voice is Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP), which requires electrical power to operate and give you 911 calling capability. A battery-backup device is available from Frontier (for an additional fee) that powers phone service for up to eight hours (when fully charged) in the event of an electric power disruption, and it maintains a life span of up to three years. FiOS® Digital Voice also is compatible with security systems.
